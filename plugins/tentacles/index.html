What It Does

  • Interactive X-Y pad — drag colored dots to place each tentacle in 2D audio space: X = stereo pan, Y = frequency band (80 Hz–12 kHz, log-scaled).
  • 4 independent tentacles — each with its own vibrato rate & depth, band-pass filter, saturation, and stereo pan.
  • Two vibrato algorithms — classic delay-line vibrato and allpass pitch-shift (Panoramatone-style).
  • Drift / chaos control — per-tentacle organic modulation of rate, pan, and filter. Subtle sway to beautifully unhinged.
  • Live visual feedback — dots pulse and glow with the real DSP LFO; ghost trails show drift paths.
  • Mono in → stereo out — feed it a mono guitar or vocal and get a wide, detailed stereo field.
  • Lightweight — near-zero CPU on all platforms.
  • VST3 format — works in every major DAW (Ableton, FL Studio, Reaper, Bitwig, Logic via wrapper, etc.).
  • Windows, macOS, Linux — all three platforms supported.
